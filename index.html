About Us

Born from the heart of Ethiopia, where blind individuals face daily
struggles due to inaccessible infrastructure, our startup emerged as a
bold solution to restore independence and dignity. We create smart,
empowering tools mobile apps, AI-powered eyeglasses, and smart canes
to help blind and low-vision people "see" their world in new ways.
Our voice guided app speaks in Amharic, offering real-time navigation cues and obstacle
alerts. Our glasses assist low-vision users with clarity and spatial awareness
, while our smart sticks sync with smartphones or AirPods to detect hazards and guide
the user safely. Together, these innovations bridge the gap between disability
and mobilityâ€”turning everyday spaces into accessible places, and every step into a confident journey.
